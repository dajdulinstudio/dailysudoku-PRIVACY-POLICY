# Privacy Policy for Sudoku Daily

**Last updated:** April 3, 2026

Sudoku Daily ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
*   **Account Information:** When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to manage your account.
*   **Game Data:** We store your Sudoku progress, statistics, streaks, and leaderboard scores in Firebase Firestore.
*   **Device Identifiers:** We use standard Android identifiers (such as Ad ID) for advertising and analytics purposes.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
*   To provide and maintain the core game experience.
*   To manage your account and sync your progress across different devices.
*   To display your chosen username and score on the public Global Leaderboard.
*   To serve advertisements via Google AdMob (personalized or non-personalized based on your consent).

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do not sell your personal data. We use the following third-party services which collect data according to their own policies:
*   **Google Firebase:** For authentication and database management.
*   **Google AdMob:** For displaying advertisements.
*   **Google User Messaging Platform (UMP):** For managing GDPR and privacy consent.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion
You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated information (stats, scores, and profile) at any time directly within the app via the **"Delete Account & Data"** button in the Profile section.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 6. GDPR and EU User Rights
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have specific rights regarding your data access and deletion. We implement the Google UMP SDK to ensure compliance with European privacy regulations.
